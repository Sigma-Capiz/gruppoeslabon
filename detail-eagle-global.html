GRUPPO ESLABON IS NOW GOING GLOBAL!

     In line with the company's vision of extending its services to our ASEAN neighbours, Gruppo Eslabon is proud to launch its new company: THE EAGLE GUARD GLOBAL SECURITY SERVICES INC. This move is in response to the ASEAN integration,in order for the company to cope with the globalization trends and to continuously build on its reputation of being among the leaders of security services in the Philippines.

     By going global, GE is aiming to extend its brand of service to regional clienteles whom are eyeing for a provider that will give them the security platform tailor fit to their company and individual needs. GE is aware of the possible risks and challenges it will encounter as it goes beyond the borders of the Philippines, cultural and language barriers are just some of the possible hurdles that the company will face, but GE believes in their capacity to break boundaries to tap a wider client market.

     BREAKING BOUNDARIES is the main battle cry of GE's decision to penerate the global market, it hopes that it will open more doors for not only the company but also for aspiring Filipino people to realize their respective global dreams.

     In line with the current trends in the global scene, GE believes that the experiences and lessons it garnered in serving the local market have prepared the group to a bigger and more diverse clientele.
