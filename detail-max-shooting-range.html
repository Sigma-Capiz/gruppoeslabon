Max Shooting Range

     Max Shooting Range (MSR) was set in motion by the GE Top management and human resources department to the appropriate venue for learning of the science and art of marksmanship, exclusively for GE and its preferred client.

     It is the home firing range of the multi-awarded GE Shooting Team. It is also the private training for GE's security personnel involved in high risk security operations. MSR is duly licensed and accredited by the PNP-Firearms and Explosive Office (FEO), and by the host local government.
