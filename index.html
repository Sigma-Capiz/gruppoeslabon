      In order to systematize and synergize the individual operations of Shooters Security Services, The 1st Eagle Guard Security Services, The Eagle Guard Global Security Services, Eslabon Security Agency, Water Dragon Protective & Detective Agency, Royal Defense Security Services, GE Armored Transportation, Secure Tech & IT Solution,and MAX Shooting Range, GRUPPO ESLABON was organized.

      Its mandate is to orchestrate the workings of the above companies to attain the lofty goal of providing the best quality services and service supports available in the security industry.

      GRUPPO ESLABON is the brainchild of creative and innovative professionals. It was designed as an umbrella organization to best serve our people, corporate, and individual companies in accordance with the principles of human and legal rights and the rule of law.

VISION

  • To be the organization of choice of quality-minded individuals and discriminating clientele in the delivery of quality service and quality people.

MISSION

  • Serve clients with the highest standards they deserve guided by foresight, experience and skills in managing risk, to the end that their assets are well protected.

CODES

  • HONOR AND LOYALTY

OBLIGATIONS

  • To protect, and if duty compels, fight even at risk of death.

Gruppo Eslabon CAREERS

Gruppo Eslabon seeks individuals with the following:

  • 01 Mandatory Qualifications for Security/Lady Guards

    Not lower than 5'7" for male and 5'2" above for female.
    College graduate or College level
    Not less than One (1) year security experience for male.
    Duly licensed by SOSIA.

  • 02 Documentary Requirements

    Set of original photocopied documents arranged as follows:
    Application Form
    Police Clearance, Baranggay, NBI, Valid ID
    SBR / Security License
    Certificate of Previous Employment
    Diploma / Transcript of Records
    Training Certificate, Opening/Closing Reports
    Authentication of Security License
    Drug and Neuro Results
    Birth Certificate
    SSS Printout/Employment History

  • 03 Receptionist (Female)

    Graduate of any 4 year course
    5'3" and above
    Fluent in English
    Must be smart and with pleasing personality
