THE BIRTH OF A WATER DRAGON!

     Water Dragon (WD) pioneers in extending comprehensive and professional Executive Protection and Security Solutions to high value persons in both the business and other VIPs in the Philippines. The company is known for providing the optimal, hassle-free environment for customers that need to focus on their business or leisure. Since its incorporation, WD's reputation has served as the bench-mark in the high standards and objectives of the security industry. The company offers proven methodology and principles that ensure clockwork precision-driving expertise in performing the tasks at hand.

     In line with Gruppo Eslabon's reputation of pioneering security services in the Philippines, the Water Dragon Protective & Detective Agency was born. The sub-agency is a brainchild of years of formulating the perfect method of offering professional protection to valuable individuals in Philippine Society. With the use of its signature security offering, WD personnel are able to give protection to customers while offering them a hassle-free environment. The agency constantly conducts skills training in order to harness its people. The tedious training sessions prepare WD personnel for the gravity of responsibility they will perform. WD offers nothing but the best personnel and lives out to its promise of providing quality service.
