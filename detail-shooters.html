SHOOTERS SECURITY SERVICES

     One of the companies under the Gruppo Eslabon is Shooters Security Services . The main identity of the entity is grounded on the drive to provide and assure quality services to a wide array of clients. It was introduced to society on July 1996, with only one client, but to date, Shooters has grown tremendously and has embedded its name as one of the leading providers of high quality security in the country. It has and is continuously serving 100 satisfied reputable firms such as deluxe hotels, residential condominiums and highrise offices, to name a few. The constant progress in their services prompted the birth of the other GE companies that the nation knows of today.

     Motivated by the desire to upgrade the quality of security service brought about by the need of the burgeoning society for well-trained, disciplined and multi-skilled security forces, the Shooters Security Services was integrated in the market. Since its inception on July 1996, the company spread its client lists from just one to over 200 clients. Shooters has been catering to a wide-array of clients including luxurious hotels, high-rise offices, residential condominiums and multinational companies. The phenomenal growth of Shooters is due mainly to its upmost commitment to personalized service and management.

     Shooters ignited the growth of GE's reputation in pioneering of security and other related services. The phenomenal growth of Shooters is said to have been pushed by their commitment in offering personalized service and management to its valued clientele. With a high 98% retention rate, it has achieved a milestone that GE is truly proud of. Shooters does not stop there. It constantly builds on experience to incorporates innovation in the services they offer. This is of course one of the main reasons why GE, together with the rest of the group, upholds excellence: they believe that everyone deserves optimum service.
